FinancePro User Guide

FinancePro – Use the floating 3D nodes to navigate the app.

Dashboard Stats – See total Income, Expenses, and Savings.

Transactions – Log every income and expense, search/filter, and export CSV.

Budget Insights – Track expense distribution, Budget vs Actual, and your Financial Health Score.

Net Worth Tracker – Enter Assets/Liabilities to calculate Net Worth. Create savings goals.

Investments – Add assets with quantity and prices to track portfolio P&L.

Subscriptions – Track fixed monthly costs and see your total burn rate.

Quick Tools – Calculators for EMI, Currency, FIRE, and Taxes.
